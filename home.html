Unique Animals
Of The Pandora Planet

The lifestyles of Na'vi clans are highly varied with their own unique traditions and beliefs, and they have developed sophisticated cultures. Despite this, they all share a profound spiritual connection to other life on their moon, each other, and an encompassing "goddess" they call Eywa.

Thanathor

These creatures are the largest top predators on land, they have the speed to ambush their prey, they are also clever hunters who are good at imitating the sounds of other creatures.

Ilu

Ilu is a species of marine animal, they are very friendly with sea tribes such as the metkayina, they are used as underwater vehicles.

Zakru

Zakru is animal has a body more than that of an african elephant, this animal is often used as a means of transporting wood by the mainland horse clan.

Dire Horse

The Direhorse is an animal that at first glance looks like a horse but has 6 strong legs, is the same height as an asian elephant, and can run for 3 days without rest.

Ikran

Ikran is one of the popular animals in pandora, used as a vehicle in the air like a plane, ikran is also used in hunting and in war, ikran only wants to fly the same person throughout his life.

Viperwolf

Viperwolf is an animal that looks lika a dog but has a voice like a hyena, this animal active at night, they live a solitary life. They are also respected by the ash people.
